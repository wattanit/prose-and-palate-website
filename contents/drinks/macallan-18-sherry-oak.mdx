<p class="dropcap">The Macallan 18 Sherry Oak stands as one of the most revered expressions in the world of single malt Scotch whisky. This iconic dram represents the pinnacle of The Macallan's sherry oak maturation process, spending eighteen years exclusively in hand-picked sherry-seasoned oak casks from Jerez, Spain.</p>

<Section heading="The Legacy" id="sec-1" variant="prose">

The Macallan has built its reputation on the quality of its oak casks, and the 18 Sherry Oak is perhaps the finest example of this philosophy. Each cask is carefully selected and seasoned with dry Oloroso sherry for approximately two years before being filled with new-make spirit.

</Section>

<Section heading="Appearance" id="sec-2" variant="prose">

Deep amber colour with rich mahogany highlights — a testament to its long maturation in sherry casks.

</Section>

<Section heading="Nose" id="sec-3" variant="prose">

Rich dried fruits — raisins, figs, and dates — intermingled with warm Christmas spices. Candied orange peel, vanilla bean, honey, and a hint of leather emerge as the whisky opens up.

</Section>

<Section heading="Palate" id="sec-4" variant="prose">

Luxuriously full-bodied with rich dried fruit flavours reminiscent of Christmas cake soaked in sherry. Warming spices of ginger, cinnamon, and nutmeg, balanced by smooth oak influence and dark chocolate notes.

</Section>

<Section heading="Finish" id="sec-5" variant="prose">

Remarkably long and evolving, with waves of dried fruit, gentle spices, and a subtle smokiness that adds intrigue without overpowering the sherry character. It is a whisky for special celebrations and contemplative evening sipping in equal measure.

</Section>
