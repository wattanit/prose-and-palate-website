Cocktail 10/10

A flawless reading of the classics — fundamentals executed so precisely that invention would only get in the way.

  • Skill 10/10

    Flawless execution across every technique, from egg white shaking to spirit-forward balance.

  • Recipe 8/10

    Classics respected at their core, with single precise adjustments that feel inevitable rather than contrived.

  • Ingredients 10/10

    Quality spirits used with full understanding of their character.

Place 9.6/10

A masterclass in the conviction that perfect fundamentals are enough.

  • Taste 10/10

    Every drink lands exactly where it intends to, with no rough edges.

  • Ambiance 10/10

    Unpretentious and comfortable; the confidence of a bar that has nothing to prove.

  • Service 10/10

    Genuinely attentive and personable; no "we are famous" vibe despite their fame.

  • Value 8/10

    Standard Singapore cocktail prices, well worth their world-class drinks.
