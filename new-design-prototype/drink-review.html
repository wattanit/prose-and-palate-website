Spirit 9.0/10

A masterclass in patience — twelve years that taught a spirit to speak softly and at length.

  • Complexity 10/10

    Layer after layer unfolds — honey, orchard fruit, toasted nut, spice — each revealing itself only to the attentive.

  • Balance 10/10

    Nothing dominates; sweetness, oak, and spirit are held in perfect, unhurried equilibrium.

  • Finish 8/10

    Long, gentle, faintly spiced — clove and orange peel — lingering ten minutes after the last sip.

  • Value 8/10

    Priced honestly for a 12-year Speysider of this calibre; not cheap, but worth every quiet dram.
